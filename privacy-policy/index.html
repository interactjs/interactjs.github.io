Privacy Policy

Last revised and updated 2019/12/26

This privacy policy will explain what data we collect about you, how we use the data, and what rights you have with respect to this data.

In short, we ask for your name and email address to sign up to our newsletter and we use google analytics with a minimal data collection configuration to measure how visitors reach and navigate the site and where they are located geographically.

We don't share any of the data we collect with anyone else and we only use the data to try to improve our user experience. You may send an email to taye@interactjs.io in case of any issues.

What data do we collect?

  1. By subscribing to our newsletter, you provide us your name and email address. There's a double opt-in confirmation email, to make sure that you intend to subscribe. If you don't confirm, your info will be removed from our database in a few days.

  2. By visiting https://interactjs.io, your browsing activity on our site will be recorded by Google Analytics. All Google Analytics data is anonymized and records of individual browsing sessions are deleted after 14 months (the shortest configurable duration). The info we collect through Google Analytics includes:

    • Geographical location
    • Browser language settings
    • The site you were referred from
    • Time and duration of visits
    • Pages visited

We do not record personal information such as age, interests and demographic through Google Analytics.

Do we share your data with anyone?

We use Mailgun to send emails and manage mailing lists and upload your name and email address to their service.

What kind of communication will we send you?

If you have signed up to our newsletter, you will receive occasional updates about the development of the project and any services we build.

Every newsletter email we send will contain a link to unsubscribe and you can email taye@interactjs.io at any time to request that we remove you from our mailing lists.

What are your data protection rights?

You are always entitled to the following rights:

  • Request access – We will provide you copies of your personal data on request without charge.
  • Request rectification – You can request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request that we complete the information you believe is incomplete.
  • Request erasure – You have the right to request that we erase your personal data where there is no good reason for us to retain it.
  • Request the restriction of processing – You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data.
  • Object to processing – You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data.
  • Data portability – You can request that we transfer the data that we have collected about you to another party.

If you'd like to exercise any of these rights, please send an email to taye@interactjs.io If you feel that we aren't respecting your rights, you may submit a complaint on the Privacy Shield website https://www.privacyshield.gov/article?id=How-to-Submit-a-Complaint.
